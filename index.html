What are the benefits of using aluminum tubing in manufacturing?
Aluminum tubing offers excellent strength-to-weight ratio, corrosion resistance, and versatility across applications including automotive, aerospace, and construction.
How is aluminum tubing produced?
Aluminum tubing is typically produced through extrusion – forcing heated aluminum billets through a shaped die – or through drawn processes for tighter tolerances.
What are the common applications of aluminum tubing?
Common applications include HVAC systems, structural framing, fluid transfer lines, medical equipment, bicycle frames, and automotive components.

Yes. Custom diameters, wall thicknesses, lengths, alloy grades, and surface finishes are all available. Contact our team for tailored specifications.

Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing Process

Our state-of-the-art extrusion technology ensures consistent quality, optimal material properties, and dimensional accuracy in every pipe we manufacture.

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity.

  • PE100 grade material
  • Optimal molecular weight distribution

Precision Extrusion

State-of-the-art single-screw extruders maintain consistent melt temperature and pressure for uniform wall thickness throughout the entire pipe length.

  • Temperature-controlled barrel zones
  • Real-time melt pressure monitoring

Controlled Cooling

Water cooling tanks rapidly solidify the pipe while maintaining precise dimensional tolerances and surface quality standards.

  • Gradual temperature reduction
  • Prevents residual stress build-up

Precision Sizing

Automatic sizing equipment ensures every pipe meets exact OD tolerance requirements per international standards.

  • Laser micrometer measurement
  • Automatic diameter correction

Rigorous Quality Control

Every pipe undergoes hydrostatic pressure testing, wall thickness checks, and visual inspection before leaving our facility.

  • Hydrostatic burst tests
  • MRS & tensile strength validation

Permanent Marking

Ink-jet printing embeds traceability data – diameter, SDR, pressure class, batch number, and certification marks – along the pipe.

  • Fade-resistant UV-stable inks
  • Compliant with IS 4984 & ISO 4427

Precision Cutting

Planetary saw cutters provide clean, burr-free ends at precise lengths for easy on-site installation and fusion joining.

  • ±1 mm length tolerance
  • Automated cut-off stations

Secure Packaging

Finished pipes are stacked, bundled, or coiled on reels depending on diameter, then wrapped for safe long-distance transport.

  • UV-stabilized polyethylene wrap
  • ISO-compliant pallet loading
